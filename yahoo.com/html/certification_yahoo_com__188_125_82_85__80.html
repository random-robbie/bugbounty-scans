Certification at Yahoo

Yahoo offers certification programs that allow you to gain and demonstrate proficiency in our core advertiser offerings: Gemini and BrightRoll DSP. After passing each exam, you'll be awarded a certification badge that you can place on your email signature, LinkedIn profile, or resume. Click one of the badges below to read more.

 
                                       

 

 

Gemini Certification

Pick the Gemini Certification option that is right for you: Native only, Search only, or Native and Search combined.

Gemini Native

This exam covers the basics of Yahoo Gemini native ads including an overview of the offering, targeting, setting bids, native video ads, app install ads, writing effective ads, reporting and more.

Gemini Search

This covers the basics of Yahoo Gemini search ads including an overview of the offering, account structure, campaign management, ad copy, ad policy, ad serving, bulk sheets, conversion tracking, reporting and more.

Gemini Native and Search Combined

If you'd like to get certified in both Gemin products in a single exam, this path is for you.

BrightRoll DSP Core Knowledge Certification

This exam covers the value proposition and core functionality of the BrightRoll DSP for self-service users. Topics include campaign setup and trafficking, optimization tactics, custom reporting, and much more.

Please note that you will be provided access to the optional certification courses as well as the required certification exam upon completing registration.

Click here to visit the BrightRoll DSP Certification homepage and get started.

 

BrightRoll DSP Advanced Certification

The goal of Advanced Certification is to expand and test the knowledge of DSP power users.  Our first Advanced Certification program focuses on Audience Builder and using Yahoo data in the BrightRoll DSP.  Sign up for Advanced Certification to gain a deep understanding of Audience Builder and how to apply custom audiences in "real world" scenarios.  

Please note that you will be provided access to the optional certification course as well as the required certification exam upon completing registration.

Click here to visit the BrightRoll DSP Certification homepage and get started.
