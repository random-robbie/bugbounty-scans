We are a proudly non-profit organization driven by a culture of openness and collaboration. It’s a value we hold strong and it’s how we work together, day in and day out.

When you join Mozilla, you’re becoming part of a global community that’s helping to build a brighter future for the Web — and for everyone.

Building & Designing

We are designers, engineers, creators and problem solvers. We work in the fishbowl known as open source, with a clear focus on making the Web better. Working with us, you’ll help build amazing things like Firefox, Rust, Firefox OS and more. To be part of the team, we ask that you be awesome, intelligent and proud to be moving the mission forward. Rock on.

Operations

We are Systems, Security, IT and Finance. Our work at Mozilla is vital to our everyday lives and it keeps you — and everyone in our community — browsing the Web, developing awesome apps and more. We also work hard to keep your identity secure, the lights on and the refrigerators filled. Yep, we do it all.

Internships

Life at Mozilla

What makes us… us.

At Mozilla, we like to find the best and most fun ways to do things, and we like to think it shows in all that we do. Throw in a good dose of freedom, teamwork and perks, and you’ve got life at Mozilla. And we haven’t even mentioned the snacks yet.

Gallery of photos showing people brainstorming, working playing, attending conferences, and working together. It also shows the personal touches many Mozillians bring to work: stuffed animals, laptop stickers, a Pac-man ghost made of post-it notes, and FireFox shoe laces.

How we take care of our people

Top-notch medical benefits

We’ll take care of you with industry leading medical, dental and vision plans and many more benefits that allow us — and you — to continue to be amazing!

Quarterly bonuses

Our bonus program is not frosting, it is part of the cake. These bonuses are available to all full-time employees who exemplify the Mozilla mission.

Remote & home working

We have 10 global offices and people working in more than 30 countries. If you work best from home, that’s not a problem. We can support you anywhere.

Personal development

We want you to grow with us. And to help you keep improving, we offer Lunch and Learns, course reimbursements, Rosetta Stone and many more opportunities for personal growth.

Office parties & cantinas

We believe that bringing people together to celebrate and socialize is a great way to inspire each other and keep our community strong and close. Party hats optional.

Snacks

Are you a latte lover? Do you crave cookies? Or do you prefer to fuel up on fruit? Whatever you need, we have it. Or we can get it.
