How we're building the world's first visual discovery engine

Solve the discovery problem

Not everything in life has a straightforward question and answer. That’s why we’re not just building a search engine, we’re building a discovery engine.

Using the world’s richest human-curated interest graph, we provide recommendations that help people discover new ideas and passions.

Be the save button for the internet

With Pinterest, now people can save ideas they find on the web or on the go. Best of all, the more they save, the more we can help them discover new ideas to try.

Help people do things they love

Most importantly, we want people to go and do all those projects and ideas they’ve discovered and saved on Pinterest.

By creating rich, useful Pins, we’re making Pinterest the best tool for planning dream vacations, trying new recipes or buying gifts for friends.

Create a platform that scales

For millions of people, using Pinterest is the best part of their day. We want to make sure it stays that way, even as millions become billions. This means creating an enduring platform that hundreds of engineers can build upon—one that maximizes flexibility, velocity and creativity.

Our open source playground has massive distributed systems, large-scale machine learning platforms and big data processing.
