The easy, conversational interface allows people to tell the bot which store location and service they want and then instantly receive the closest dates and times. People can also book the appointment completely in Messenger, and instantly receive a confirmation email.

+11% increase in booking rate through Sephora Reservation Assistant vs. other booking vehicles
