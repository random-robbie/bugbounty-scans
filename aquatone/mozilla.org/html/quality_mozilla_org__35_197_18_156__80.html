Get Involved

The Mozilla QA team drives software quality assurance activities across Mozilla and plays a key role in releasing a diverse range of software products on schedule.

  • Contribute to our quality assurance activities by filing bugs and testing features.
  • Attend our public meeting held every other Wednesday at 1:30 PM PST.
  • Talk to our team members in our public chat room.
  • Find all our documentation on the QA Zone of the Mozilla Developer Network.
  • Join our mailing list where you can participate in ongoing discussions.
  • Visit our Mozilla QA Discourse forum and start a discussion
  • Subscribe to the RSS feed of our blog to be notified of news and upcoming events.
