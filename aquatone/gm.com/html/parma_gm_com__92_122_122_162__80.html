Welcome to Parma

General Motors Parma Metal Center processes over 1,000 tons of steel per day and services/supports approximately 40 customers including the majority of General Motors North America produced vehicles. Parma has over 1,400 total dies and is capable of producing up to 100-million parts per year, including 56-million parts in 2014. The manufacturing processes include small, medium, and large transfer press lines, high speed progressive presses, and a world class Cut To Length shear, as well as GMNA’s largest stand-alone, multi-cell, resistance welding metal assembly operations. The Parma Metal Center is located near Interstate 480 between West 130th Street and Tiedeman Road, just east of Cleveland Hopkins International Airport.

GM PARMA Earns Wildlife Habitat Certification

2014-11-12

General Motors Parma Metal Center has been recognized by the Wildlife Habitat Council for enhancing biodiversity at the facility and engaging community partners to promote wildlife conservation and education. The plant received a Wildlife at Work certification for replacing obsolete tank farms with a native prairie meadow and installing duck and bat houses in field and wooded areas.
