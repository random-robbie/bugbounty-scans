Part of the global General Motors organisation,
GM Power Components is a brand new operation set up specifically to supply engines, transmissions and other components to accredited Original Equipment Manufacturers. Working in conjunction with our approved partner Millbrook, we provide a one-stop shop for all your parts, service and engineering needs.

 As the world's largest vehicle manufacturer,
General Motors has carried out massive investment in the development of high quality, cost-efficient components. At the same time, our vast experience has given us a special insight into the needs of the individual Original Equipment Manufacturer.

Customers of GM Power Components can buy into this investment and expertise and, in doing so, reduce their exposure to the potentially crippling R&D costs inherent in developing any new product, thereby freeing up capital within your organisation.

We can also help reduce development times and streamline component supply, allowing you to hit the market with the Right Product at the Right Time.
