What is Maven+ ?

Maven+ is an exclusive car-sharing program for residential communities. Approved participants will have 24/7 access to a variety of vehicles, conveniently parked in the related residential community parking garages. Vehicles can be checked out via our mobile app for use anywhere in the United States. Date night? Weekend getaway? Grocery trip? Business trip? Find the right car at the right time with Maven+.

Program Details

  • Exclusive car-sharing program for qualified residents (invite only) at any of our partner residential buildings
  • Parking is included in the usage fee at the designated garage locations listed in the mobile app for your community
  • Vehicle insurance and fuel for your trip is included in the usage fee
  • No membership fee - Free to join
  • Hourly rates start at $9/hr up to 8 hours of usage
  • Daily rate starts at $75/day for over 8 hours up to 24 hours
  • Variety of vehicle types available featuring SUVs, luxury and sedans
