Booking an infant?
If your infant does not require a separate air seat, please contact our customer care center at 1-888-854-3899 for assistance with your booking. If you wish to reserve a separate air seat for your infant, please enter the age of the infant as 1 and make sure you bring proof of your child's age to the airport. Children ages 2 or older have to purchase a separate air seat.
