FEDSIM: Shaping The Future Of Acquisition

FEDSIM is a Client Support Center housed in the General Services Administration’s Office of Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS). FEDSIM is the leading provider of assisted acquisition services for federal agencies. Our organization provides hands-on acquisition, financial, and project management for clients throughout the life cycle of their acquisitions.

Why choose FEDSIM?

Services

We provide continuous, hands-on, mission-centric program, financial, and acquisition management expertise across the life cycle of each contract.

SOLUTIONS

We customize our approach to each individual project and use our trusted processes and business practices to drive high-quality outcomes.

SAVINGS

We handle all the details. We save our clients time and money while they focus on what’s important—their agency's mission.

SKILLS

We are not just acquisition, financial, and project management experts; we are trusted advisors and client advocates.
